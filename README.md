###A Global Startup by Abdelkarim Elfar
ElfaGo is more than a delivery app — it’s a global lifestyle platform built to be smarter, faster, and more versatile than DoorDash, Uber Eats, or Deliveroo.

AI‑powered logistics: Machine learning predicts demand and optimizes routes for faster, more reliable service.
Drone delivery vision: Future drone stations will deliver meals in minutes, reduce traffic, and even save lives by transporting essentials in emergencies.
Global reach: Rooted in Morocco, expanding to Senegal and Germany, with a brand designed for worldwide scalability.
Better than competitors: Unlike other apps, ElfaGo unites food, transport, and future tech under one sleek brand.

ElfaGo is a startup created by Abdelkarim Elfar, built to redefine how people connect with meals, mobility, and technology — everywhere.
